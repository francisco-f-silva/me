About me.

Software Engineer

While finishing my studies in Medicine, I realized it wasn’t what I wanted to do for the next years of my life. From there on, my interest in problem solving led me to programming and, not long after, I was working on being a Software Engineer.

These last years I’ve been studying and gaining some experience in the field (more info on LinkedIn). So now I’m looking for a bigger challenge and intend to go work and live abroad from Portugal, something I’ve wanted to experience for a while after having been abroad for some months.

I feel eager to learn, to be welcomed into a team and to give my contribution for a project I identify with. I aim at doing things the best I can, and still feel I need to prove something to myself. So I expect nothing from me than to give my best, wherever I am. My greatest hobby is playing padel, a sport with some similarities to tennis. You play it in doubles and it's the most addictive sport ever!
